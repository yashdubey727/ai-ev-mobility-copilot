# EV Road Trip Knowledge Base (Prototype)

## Charging best practices
- Fast charging slows down above ~80% SOC; for road trips, charging to 70–80% is often time-optimal.
- Maintain a safety buffer (10–15% SOC) to handle detours, traffic, wind, elevation, and charger outages.
- Prefer high-power fast chargers (150kW+) when minimizing charging time.

## Sustainability guidance
- Driving speed has a large impact on EV efficiency; reducing highway speed can extend range materially.
- Smooth acceleration and steady speed improve efficiency.
- Minimizing detours reduces energy use.

## Safety and reliability
- Avoid planning arrivals below 8–10% SOC unless there are known reliable chargers near the destination.
- If charger availability is uncertain, increase buffer and plan earlier stops.
